# Ask Your Business - Period Readiness & Certification Checklist v1.4

This zero-dependency Railway demo builds on v1.3 and adds a period-level readiness workflow.

## What v1.4 adds

- New **Period Readiness** tab.
- Separate readiness gates for **Management-ready** and **Accountant-certified** status.
- Checklist covering company setup, open owner questions, review blockers, Serbian konto/posting/tax completion, validation/control review, and data quality.
- Month readiness board for March, April, and May.
- Exportable period certification pack.
- Existing individual review, Serbian COA dictionary, source evidence viewer, and certified data views remain intact.

## Important

This demo is still browser-local. Review decisions, company setup, period statuses, and certification decisions are saved in localStorage. Export the certified/certification package before clearing browser data.

Test deployment with `/health`. Expected version: `1.4.0`.
